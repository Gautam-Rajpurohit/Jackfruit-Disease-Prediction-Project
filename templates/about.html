About Us

Welcome to the Jackfruit Disease Detection project! Our goal is to provide a reliable and efficient tool for farmers and researchers to identify diseases affecting jackfruit plants.

Utilizing advanced machine learning techniques, particularly a pre-trained ResNet model, we aim to classify jackfruit leaf images into categories such as healthy, algae spot, and black spot.

By leveraging this technology, we hope to enhance crop management and support the agricultural community in combating plant diseases.
